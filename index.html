About Me

Research Experience

WED-Net: Weather-Effect Disentanglement for Urban Flow Prediction
Advisor: Prof. Xiao Zhou  ·  Renmin University of China  ·  2025 – 2026

Qian Hong, Siyuan Chang, Xiao Zhou

Proposed a novel disentanglement network that separates weather-induced effects from intrinsic urban flow patterns using causal augmentation. The model significantly improves generalization under adverse weather conditions. The work was accepted at WWW 2026, arXiv:2601.22586.

Controllable Affective Generation via Latent Vector Steering
Advisor: Prof. Xiao Zhou  ·  Renmin University of China  ·  2025 – 2026

Xixian Yong, Siyuan Chang, Xiao Zhou, Zihe Wang, Yingying Zhang, Yefeng Zheng, Xian Wu

Investigated latent-space steering techniques for fine-grained control of affective attributes in generated text without task-specific fine-tuning. The paper is currently under review at ACL 2026.
